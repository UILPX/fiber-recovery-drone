# Security Policy

## Reporting safety or security concerns

Please do not open public issues for vulnerabilities, unsafe deployment details,
or misuse-enabling findings.

Report concerns privately through GitHub private vulnerability reporting if it is
enabled for this repository, or contact the repository owner directly through
GitHub.

## Scope

Reports are in scope if they involve:

- Unsafe control behavior or missing stop conditions.
- Excessive pulling force, unexpected motor startup, or loss of emergency stop.
- Privacy exposure in field videos or logs.
- Misuse-enabling documentation, designs, or instructions.
- Supply-chain risks in procurement recommendations.

## Out of scope

- Requests to weaponize, evade regulation, bypass safety checks, or deploy in
  hazardous areas without qualified review.
- Requests for operational advice in active conflict, policing, or surveillance
  contexts.

## Disclosure

The owner may remove, redact, or delay publication of details that could enable
unsafe replication or misuse.
