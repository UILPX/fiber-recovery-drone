# Responsible Use Policy

This project is intended for peaceful disaster recovery, post-conflict
reconstruction, communications cleanup, and controlled engineering research.

## Allowed project direction

- Small-scale indoor or closed-field prototypes.
- Human-supervised cable recovery experiments.
- Low-force tension sensing and stop logic.
- Documentation, safety review, procurement planning, and validation tests.
- Human-assisted untangling simulation before any autonomous or aerial work.

## Disallowed project direction

Contributions, forks, derivative designs, or deployment plans must not add or
support:

- Weapons, targeting, tracking of people, or hostile operations.
- Cutting, burning, explosive, high-force, or destructive end effectors.
- Tools for moving, disturbing, or manipulating suspected unexploded ordnance.
- Autonomous operation in uncleared dangerous areas.
- Operation near energized power lines, substations, rail electrification, or
  public crowds.
- Surveillance or privacy-invasive data collection.
- Instructions that bypass legal, safety, flight, or site-access requirements.

## Review expectations

Any change that increases autonomy, pulling force, payload, flight contact,
deployment realism, or field capability must also update:

- `docs/safety_and_ethics.md`
- `operations/field_workflow.md`
- `tests/validation_plan.md`

The project owner may reject or remove contributions that create unacceptable
safety, misuse, legal, or reputational risk.
